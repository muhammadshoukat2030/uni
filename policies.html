Library Policies

Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. The University Digital Library collects and uses your data solely to provide and improve our services.

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent except as required by law.

By using our library, you agree to our data handling practices described in this policy.

Terms of Use

Users are granted access to the digital resources for academic and personal use only. Redistribution, commercial use, or unauthorized sharing of content is prohibited.

You agree to use the library resources responsibly and not to engage in activities that compromise the security or functionality of the system.

  • Access is limited to current students, faculty, and staff.
  • Do not attempt to bypass or tamper with security measures.
  • Respect copyright and intellectual property laws.
  • Report any suspicious activity or content to library support immediately.

Library Rules

To ensure a positive experience for all users, please adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Maintain academic integrity when using resources.
  • Keep login credentials confidential and secure.
  • Do not share accounts or passwords with others.
  • Contact library staff for any help or issues with access.
  • Use resources ethically and cite appropriately in your work.
